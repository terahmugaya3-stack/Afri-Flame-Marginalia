About the collection

A shelf, kept properly

Marginalia started as a single shelf of paperbacks lent out to friends, tracked in the back of a notebook. It's grown into a small catalogued library — every book given a call number, a condition note, and a place on the list.

Some titles are here to be borrowed for free and passed along. Others are duplicates or finished collections offered for sale, priced to move rather than to profit.

A library card (a free account) lets you reserve a free title or purchase one for sale. The librarian's desk posts updates here whenever new arrivals hit the stacks.
